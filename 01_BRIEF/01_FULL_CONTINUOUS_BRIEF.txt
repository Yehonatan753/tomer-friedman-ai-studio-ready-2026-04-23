OFFERS / PRICING COMMUNICATION STRATEGY
The offer section must communicate five layers, not just price cards.

Layer 1 - Why this offer exists in the ladder
- TF Tracker = independent entry product, not a fake teaser
- BodyMetRiX Lite = self-diagnosis starting point
- Online process = remote flagship
- Full personal guidance = highest-intensity premium tier
- Measurement card = maintenance / continuation product
- Pro product = for professionals, not for the regular buyer of personal guidance

Layer 2 - Value stack
For flagship offers, restore quantified value-stack communication:
- questionnaire and diagnostic review
- full strategy session
- adaptive nutrition plan
- workout plan
- daily WhatsApp support
- monthly measurements
- full TF Tracker usage
- total market value
- actual Tomer price

Layer 3 - Market comparison
Use premium framing, not spammy hype.
Explain what similar premium support usually costs and why Tomer's offer is positioned strongly.

Layer 4 - Urgency / scarcity
Use operational scarcity only.
No fake countdowns.
Examples:
- limited number of new clients per month
- limited number of active personal slots at one time
- waiting period once slots are full

Layer 5 - What happens after
Use a small continuation panel that shows renewal / next step logic without dumping ugly internal details.

Critical rule:
Do not flatten this section into sterile pricing cards.
This section must sell value intelligently.
