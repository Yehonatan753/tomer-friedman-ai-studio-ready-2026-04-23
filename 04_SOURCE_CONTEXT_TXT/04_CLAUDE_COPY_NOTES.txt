## Issues Left Intentionally Untouched

### InsuranceBanner — hardcoded in component

The headline "אולי מגיע לך הרבה כסף!" reads as clickbait and could conflict with the "no fake urgency" rule. However, this is hardcoded in `InsuranceBanner.tsx`, not in `data.ts`, and the brief says not to edit layout/styling components unless absolutely required. **Flag for a separate component pass.**

The CTA "בדוק זכאותך" links to `#pathfinder`. This is a valid destination since the Pathfinder quiz collects contact info and passes answers to Tomer, enabling a real eligibility check. No change needed.

### Pathfinder — fully hardcoded in component

All Pathfinder questions, recommendation logic, and result copy live in `Pathfinder.tsx`. The current intro already says clearly: "המטרה היא לכוון אותך למוצר או למסלול שבאמת נכון לשלב שאתה נמצא בו." This is honest and clear. No data.ts changes possible here.

### Testimonials — already appropriately scoped

All 4 testimonials are anonymous, first-person, and carry honest result tags ("התמדה ושינוי הרגלים", "סדר באכילה ובמעקב"). None overclaim. The `approved: false` flag on 3 of them correctly triggers the "ההמלצה מוצגת בעילום שם" disclaimer in the component. No changes made.

### Offers data — no changes

Pricing, includes lists, and renewal terms are accurate per the April 19, 2026 pricing document. No copy changes were made to offers themselves, only to the audience framing of TF Tracker.

### cartBonuses — not changed

These appear to serve a different display context (possibly checkout/cart summary). They overlap with `entryCard.checklist` but are likely shown in a different flow. Left as-is to avoid breaking downstream usage without understanding the full context.
